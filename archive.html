My keyboard Archive:

This is an archive for my client work and own personal collection/projects.

Client projects:

First Commmission: TOFU65 kit with Kailh Box Navies:

This was my first commission I have ever done. Keyboard was tuned to the customer's taste, the stabilisers were lubed and inside case was filled with a sheet of foam for a deeper typing sound experience.

GX64 with lubed Gateron Yellows:

Second commission for a very very close friend of mine. Keyswitches were lubed with Tribosys 3203 and stabilisers were lubed with Tribosys 3203 and Multi-purpose synthetic grease, were also clipped.

Third Commission: Modded Miya Pro Moonlight with MOD-M switches:

This commission had proven to be quite a long and difficult task to complete. As the customer requested that the stock switches were to be replaced by MOD-M switches.

Personal collection/projects:

75% kit with Zealios V2 67g:

My first kit that I assembled myseslf. Switches lubed with Tribosys 3203 and stabilisers lubed with Tribosys 3203 and Multi-purpose synthetic grease, were also clipped.

Ergodox with Kailh Copper Switches:

My first ergonomic keyboard added to my collection. Interesting typing experience with a big learning curve in order to obtain near my average typing speed.

Unicomp Model M:

Recreation model of the original IBM Model M. Awesome typing experience for those who enjoy annoying their housemates.
